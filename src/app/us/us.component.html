Our story

How It Started

Class Design's story began over 40 years ago in the picturesque region of Andalucia, Spain, rooted in the bathroom manufacturing industry. With a passion for bringing exceptional European design and quality into every home, we expanded our reach to the United States over 12 years ago. The inspiration behind Class Design stems from a deep-seated belief in creating furniture that becomes the heart of a family home, weaving warmth and togetherness into every piece.

Our journey was not without its challenges. Translating the love and craftsmanship of our dedicated team into products that resonate globally required perseverance and dedication. Every milestone, from making our first client happy to reaching customers in far-off places like Hawaii and the Seychelles, has shaped Class Design into the beloved brand it is today. Our partnerships with talented developers and artists have further enriched our offerings, allowing us to continuously bring the best European designs to homes around the world.

Our Craftsmen and Craftswomen

At the heart of Class Design are our skilled craftsmen and craftswomen, whose passion and expertise bring each piece of furniture to life.

  • Mari Carmen: A 61-year-old artisan whose meticulous hand-painting of gold leaf details adds a touch of elegance to our beds and other intricate furniture pieces.
  • Louisa: A 55-year-old expert who carefully selects the finest pieces of leather to upholster chairs and sofas, ensuring perfection in every stitch.

Our craftsmanship philosophy is defined by the people behind each product. Skills are passed down from generation to generation, preserving traditional techniques while embracing innovative approaches. Our craftsmen and craftswomen contribute to the innovation and quality of our products through a meticulous process that involves several stages of manufacturing, with little to no use of mass production techniques. Each piece is quality-checked multiple times and hand-painted to ensure excellence. Class Design’s commitment to quality and durability is evident in our careful selection of materials and the rigorous crafting process. The personal stories and achievements of our artisans embody the values and mission of Class Design, reflecting the love, dedication, and integrity that go into every piece of furniture. Our craftsmen and craftswomen are not just creators; they are the heart and soul of Class Design, ensuring that every piece we produce is a work of art meant to be cherished for generations.
